About me

For the past 3 years,I have been studying Computing in CIT, the course covers a wide variety of subjects with an emphasis on java programming of which by the end of my third year I will have covered 8 modules worth, including a module in android app development. Other modules covered include Web Development, Database Development, IT management and Requirements engineering. In my spare time, I am also developing a professional résumé website.
