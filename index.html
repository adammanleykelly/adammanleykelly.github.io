About me

For the past 3 years,I have been studying Computing in CIT, the course covers a wide variety of subjects with an emphasis on java programming of which by the end of my third year I will have covered 8 modules worth, including a module in android app development. Other languages covered in the course are HTML, CSS and Java-script. I also have a small amount of experience with C from my time attending the Munster programming training program when I was in secondary school.

Along with programming the course focuses on topics like Networking and Systems Administration, the various networking modules were all based off of Cisco’s courses, and Systems Administration courses were on Linux systems. Other modules covered include Web Development, Database Development, IT management and Requirements engineering. In my spare time, I am also developing a professional résumé website.
